Content

Home page

Welcome to Pho Saigon, where you can find delicious Vietnamese noodle soups of many varieties and flavors. We focus on providing our customers with great satisfaction. Our products are fresh and healthy, with high-quality ingredients. With our unique authentic traditional Vietnamese recipes, your palate will be satisfied as you choose Pho Saigon. This website offers online ordering capabilities to those who like Vietnamese food or noodles in general. The website is easy to use with clear instructions on how to order online, how to make a payment, how to contact us, how to track your orders, and so on. Our Pho Saigon not only wants to introduce our signature Pho(s) dish, but also other food that belongs to our distinctive Vietnamese culinary tradition. As you look at our menu, you can see it is filled with different options for different types of Pho. In addition to our main dish, our restaurant also provides a variety of other Vietnamese cuisines such as: spring rolls (goi cuon), fried spring rolls (cha gio), Vietnamese baguette (banh mi), charcoal-grilled pork on rice noodles (bun thit nuong), fried rice, desserts, and so much more. For those who are not familiar with Vietnamese cuisine, our website provides clear images with detailed food descriptions.

Menu

APPETIZERS: Fried spring rolls (cha gio): Made from pork, shrimp, carrots, and more all wrapped in rice paper and fried, this dish is sure to delight your tastebuds! Fresh spring rolls (goi cuon): Also with pork and shrimp, but with a cacophony of fresh veggies, these ingredients are wrapped tightly together with rice noodles in wet rice paper and served with spicy fish sauce. NOODLE SOUP: Beef noodles soup (pho bo): This soup includes beef, rice noodles, and a hot savory broth with a variety of herbs, and bean sprouts. Chicken noodles soup (pho ga): This soup has with chicken toppings, aromatic spices, rice noodles, sprinkled with some herbs, onions, and green onions. DESSERT: Thailand Fruit Cocktail (che Thai): This includes some fresh fruits such as: jackfruit slices, green jelly, longans, lychees, coconut milk and more. Ching Po Leung (sam bo luong): This includes sliced seaweed, lotus seeds, longans, and red jujubes. VERMICELLI: Grilled pork on rice noodles (bun thit nuong): Grilled pork, pickles, raw vegetables, roasted peanuts, and sour-sweet chili garlic fish sauce. Grilled pork and fried spring rolls with rice noodles (bun cha gio): Grilled pork, fried spring rolls, pickles, raw vegetables, roasted peanuts, and sour-sweet chili garlic fish sauce. RICE: Fried rice with veggies (com chien): Fried rice with sausages, seafood, carrots, peas, and eggs. Broken rice (com tam): Grilled pork chops, fried egg, cucumber and tomato slices, a steamed egg cake, steamed white rice, served with sweet, sour, and spicy fish sauce.

About us

Hi! My name is Ly! I am from Vietnam, and I absolutely love to cook. For years after I moved to the United States, I missed the traditional, authentic taste of Vietnamese cuisine. It is very hard to find good restaurants that have that pure taste of home for me. Not only that, I want my children to also have the sensation of what real Vietnamese food should taste like. So, I decided to roll up my sleeves and learn how to cook my favorite dishes! I have done all the research and the more I threw myself into it, the more passionate I have become about cooking. These Southeast Asian flavors take me back to Saigon, the city of my birth, and let me feel that home-cooked fragrant taste again. For years now, I have cooked for my family, and they have all been so satisfied with the food that they encouraged me to open a restaurant. The journey has been a long and difficult one, but it has led me to open this online shop. Now, I can offer a taste of Vietnamese Pho noodles and soups to you and your family too! Feel free to contact me below if you have any additional questions about my menu.
